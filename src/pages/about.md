![Asha Media House](public/asha-media-house-og.png)

**Asha Media House** is a digital media and technology organization dedicated to responsible journalism, community storytelling, and modern digital infrastructure.

Founded with the belief that local voices deserve strong representation, Asha Media House focuses on delivering timely, accurate, and meaningful news while also building the digital platforms that support modern communication and storytelling.

---

## Our Mission

Our mission is simple:

- Report with accuracy and responsibility  
- Strengthen community awareness  
- Highlight stories that matter locally  
- Build reliable digital platforms for communities and organizations  

We believe journalism is not only about breaking news, but about documenting society, encouraging dialogue, and empowering citizens with reliable information.

---

## What We Do

Asha Media House operates at the intersection of **media and technology**, combining journalism with modern digital infrastructure.

Our work includes:

- Hyperlocal digital journalism  
- Community storytelling and reporting  
- Web platform development  
- Digital infrastructure for media and organizations  

This integrated approach allows us to support both **public-interest journalism** and **the digital growth of local institutions and businesses**.

---

## Our Network

Asha Media House functions as a growing ecosystem of media and digital platforms.

### Bidire News  
Our first operational newsroom, **Bidire News**, is a hyperlocal digital publication serving Moodabidire and surrounding communities.

👉 https://www.bidirenews.com

Bidire News focuses on civic developments, education, local events, and community-driven reporting.

---

### Asha Web Studio  
**Asha Web Studio** is the technology and digital services division of Asha Media House.

👉 https://ashawebstudio.com

The studio designs and develops modern websites and digital platforms for businesses, organizations, and media initiatives. It also provides the technological infrastructure powering projects within the Asha Media House network.

## Editorial Values

Our editorial philosophy is guided by three core principles:

- **Accuracy before speed**  
- **Community before virality**  
- **Responsibility before opinion**

Every report published under Asha Media House reflects these values.

## Our Vision

We envision a future where **hyperlocal journalism and digital innovation grow together** — where communities remain informed, connected, and heard.

Asha Media House aims to build a trusted regional media platform while developing the technological infrastructure that helps organizations and communities thrive online.
